Our Ongoing Projects

Street Kids Feeding Program

We provide daily, nutritious meals to children living on the streets—meals that do more than satisfy hunger. Each plate served is a step toward recovery, helping these vulnerable children regain their strength, restore their dignity, and believe again in a future filled with hope and opportunity

Temporary Shelter Program

We offer safe, temporary shelters for children rescued from the streets, giving them a chance to rebuild their lives. Due to limited space, each member of our team currently fosters up to three children, providing them with care and stability until a dedicated facility is secured.

Education Support Initiative

We support street-connected children by covering school tuition, providing uniforms, and supplying essential learning materials—removing the barriers that keep them out of the classroom and giving them a chance to learn, grow, and dream again.

Our Upcoming Projects

Skill Training Workshops

Our aim is to offer practical skills like carpentry, tailoring, computer literacy, and soap making to help street youth become self-reliant.Also teach communication and other soft skills to build confidence, responsibility, and a brighter future.

Community Engagement Initiative

We engage local communities in caring for street children, creating a shared sense of responsibility and a safer, more supportive environment for every child to grow and thrive.

Advocacy and Awareness Campaign

We raise awareness about the struggles faced by street-connected children and advocate for their rights, ensuring their voices are heard and their dignity is protected in every space—from local communities to national conversations.
